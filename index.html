Data Pulse


Data Pulse keeps track of a stream of information. It enables you to get a sense of the state of your data by setting colors that represent different conditions.

In this case we are calculating the average amount of social media information, streamed in every 3 seconds, and comparing it to the previous 3 seconds. The difference of information streamed is represented by the pulse and color.

  • -5 to 5
  • greater than 5
